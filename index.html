SHINE AS STARS

At Tara – Shine Lives, we believe that coding is not just a skill, but a superpower that can transform lives. Our mission is to empower students—especially girls and underrepresented communities—with the tools and confidence to build their future in technology. Through free workshops, mentorship programs, and real-world projects, we create a supportive environment where young minds learn to code, innovate, and lead. Whether you're a beginner or aspiring developer, Tara is here to help you rise, shine, and succeed in the world of tech.
